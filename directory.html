these are all of my currently completed web projects:

the clock

the clock is a simple page with various clocks on it. currently working on a clock with hands, using the canvas.

colour change

click the coloured boxes to change the colour of the center box. Click the arrow to apply gradients.

coin flip

the coin flip is the page that inspired this website. on clicking a button, the coin will flip, and randomly decide heads or tails.

dice roller

various dice of various sizes (d6, d12, d20) can be clicked to be rolled, and will return a random value.

string

a collection of different conversions applied to an inputted string, including reversal, vowel counts, and more.

starry night

a starry night and stars are falling. when they collide (with each other or the floor) they explode into little stars.

bubbles

bubbles exist in a contained space and bounce off both the edges of the container, and each other.

maths

this is a sort of maths cheat sheet for simple mathematical tasks, such as powers and derivatives.

algorithms

a collection of functional sorting algoritms taught to me during my computer science course.

fibonacci

a visual example of how the fibonacci sequence works, including a linear graph.

sum until one

this function adds together the digits of any number until there is only one digit total

hex codes

hex codes are the computer values assigned to colours. there are many different ways for hex codes to be constructed.
